<p><p>The Infantry Fighting Vehicles (IFV) market is divided into two main types: crawler and wheel types. Crawler-type IFVs utilize tracked systems, providing enhanced off-road mobility and stability, making them ideal for rugged terrains and combat environments. In contrast, wheel-type IFVs leverage wheeled configurations, offering higher speed and maneuverability on paved roads, making them suitable for urban operations. Both types cater to different operational needs, with crawler types excelling in rough terrain and wheel types facilitating rapid deployment over varied landscapes.</p></p>
