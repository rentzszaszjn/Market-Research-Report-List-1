<p><p>The concrete services market is primarily divided into lightweight and heavy concrete types, each serving distinct applications. Lightweight concrete, made using lightweight aggregates, offers improved insulation and reduced structural load, making it ideal for high-rise buildings and precast elements. In contrast, heavy concrete, incorporating dense aggregates, is designed for applications requiring enhanced strength and durability, such as foundations and protective barriers. Each type addresses specific engineering needs, catering to various construction projects and enhancing overall structural integrity.</p></p>
