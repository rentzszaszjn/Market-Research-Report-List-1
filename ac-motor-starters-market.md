<p><ul><li>Reduced Voltage AC Starters</li><li>Full Voltage AC Motor Starters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC motor starters are essential devices that manage the operation of AC motors. The market is primarily segmented into two types: reduced voltage AC starters and full voltage AC motor starters. Reduced voltage starters, such as auto-transformer or star-delta starters, minimize inrush current and mechanical stress during motor startup, enhancing longevity. Full voltage starters provide direct, full power to the motor immediately, suitable for applications requiring constant speed and power. Each type serves distinct applications based on operational needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
