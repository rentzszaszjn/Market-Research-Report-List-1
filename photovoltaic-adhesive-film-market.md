<p><p>The photovoltaic adhesive film market comprises three main types: transparent EVA film, white EVA film, and POE film. Transparent EVA film offers excellent light transmission and bonding properties, making it ideal for high-efficiency solar panels. White EVA film reflects heat, enhancing energy output while providing protection against UV rays. POE film, with its superior durability and weather resistance, is gaining traction due to its long lifespan and efficiency in various environmental conditions. Each type serves distinct needs in solar module manufacturing.</p></p>
