<p><p>The Foundry Waste Sand Recycling market encompasses three key types: Clay Sand, Resin Sand, and Water Glass Sand. Clay Sand is traditional and widely used for its cost-effectiveness and recyclability. Resin Sand, known for its superior casting quality, utilizes synthetic resins for binding, making it ideal for precision applications. Water Glass Sand employs sodium silicate as the binder, offering a more environmentally friendly alternative. Each type presents unique recycling processes and applications, contributing to sustainability in the foundry industry.</p></p>
