<p><p>Ball cameras are versatile surveillance devices widely used for security. The market is primarily segmented into analog and IP cameras. Analog cameras transmit video signals through coaxial cables, offering reliable performance and straightforward installation, but with limited resolution. In contrast, IP cameras utilize internet protocol networks for higher resolution, remote access, and advanced features like motion detection. Both types serve distinct needs, with analog cameras appealing to those prioritizing cost-effectiveness, while IP cameras cater to users seeking modern, flexible surveillance solutions.</p></p>
