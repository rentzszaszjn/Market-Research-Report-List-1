<p><p>Micro-Magnetic Resonance Imaging (μMRI) encompasses various MRI types tailored for specific applications and user preferences. Open MRI features a wide, open design for easier patient access and comfort, while Closed MRI offers a traditional cylindrical shape for higher resolution imaging. Standard Bore MRI has a standard-sized opening suitable for most imaging needs, whereas Wide-Bore MRI provides a larger opening for larger patients and those with claustrophobia, balancing comfort and advanced imaging capabilities. Each type serves distinct diagnostic requirements in healthcare settings.</p></p>
