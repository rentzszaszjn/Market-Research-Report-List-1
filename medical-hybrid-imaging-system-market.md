<p><strong>The Medical Hybrid Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PET/CT</li><li>SPECT/CT</li><li>PET/MRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical hybrid imaging system market encompasses technologies like PET/CT, SPECT/CT, and PET/MRI, each combining different imaging modalities for enhanced diagnostic capabilities. PET/CT integrates positron emission tomography with computed tomography, offering detailed metabolic and anatomical information, ideal for oncology. SPECT/CT combines single-photon emission computed tomography with CT, enhancing functional imaging for various conditions, including cardiology and neurology. PET/MRI merges the strengths of positron emission tomography with magnetic resonance imaging, providing high-resolution images with superior soft tissue contrast for complex diagnoses.</p></p>
