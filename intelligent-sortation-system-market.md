<p><p>The Intelligent Sortation System market encompasses various configurations, primarily Linear and Loop Sortation Systems. Linear Sortation Systems operate with a straight design, utilizing gravity or conveyors to efficiently direct items to designated areas, ideal for high-speed operations. In contrast, Loop Sortation Systems feature a closed-loop design, allowing items to circulate within the system for sorting and distribution. Both systems enhance operational efficiency, improve accuracy, and are critical for sectors such as e-commerce, logistics, and warehousing.</p></p>
