<p><p>The optical fiber array market is categorized primarily into 1D and 2D fiber arrays. 1D fiber arrays consist of a linear arrangement of optical fibers, typically used for applications requiring single-dimensional data transmission, such as in telecommunications and sensors. In contrast, 2D fiber arrays feature a two-dimensional layout, enabling more complex data handling and processing, often utilized in imaging systems, photonic circuits, and advanced communication networks. Both types are essential for enhancing connectivity and data throughput in various industries.</p></p>
