<p><ul><li>OTDR</li><li>OFDR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed fibre optics sensing technology encompasses various methods for measuring physical parameters over long distances using optical fibres. Two primary market types are Optical Time Domain Reflectometry (OTDR) and Optical Frequency Domain Reflectometry (OFDR). OTDR utilizes time-domain techniques to detect reflected light and measure distance, making it suitable for locating faults or monitoring conditions in real-time. OFDR, on the other hand, employs frequency-domain techniques for higher resolution measurements, enabling precise monitoring of temperature, strain, and other parameters in complex environments.</p></p>
