<p><p>The double-angle milling cutter market comprises three main types based on angles: 45 degrees, 60 degrees, and 90 degrees. The 45-degree angle cutters are often used for making V-shaped grooves and bevels, while 60-degree angle cutters are ideal for creating acute angles and superior finishes in intricate components. On the other hand, 90-degree angle cutters are primarily utilized for making sharp corners and precise edges in machining operations. Each type serves specific applications across various industries, enhancing efficiency and precision.</p></p>
