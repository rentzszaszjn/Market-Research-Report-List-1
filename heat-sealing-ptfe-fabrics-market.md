<p><strong>The Heat Sealing PTFE Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Porous PTFE</li><li>Non-Porous PTFE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat sealing PTFE fabrics market is primarily divided into two types: porous and non-porous PTFE. Porous PTFE fabrics allow gases and vapors to pass through while maintaining a barrier against liquids, making them suitable for applications requiring breathability. In contrast, non-porous PTFE fabrics provide a complete barrier, offering superior resistance to chemicals and moisture, ideal for protective clothing and seals. Both types cater to diverse industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and industrial applications, emphasizing durability and performance.</p></p>
