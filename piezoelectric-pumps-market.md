<p><p>The piezoelectric pumps market is primarily categorized into standard piezoelectric pumps and large capacity piezoelectric pumps. Standard piezoelectric pumps are designed for applications requiring precise fluid control and smaller volumes, making them ideal for medical devices and microfluidics. In contrast, large capacity piezoelectric pumps cater to industrial and high-flow applications, offering greater throughput for processes like chemical dosing and water treatment. Both types leverage piezoelectric materials to convert electrical energy into mechanical motion for efficient fluid movement.</p></p>
