<p><strong>The Shaft Couplings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexible Couplings</li><li>Rigid Couplings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shaft couplings market comprises two main types: flexible couplings and rigid couplings. Flexible couplings are designed to accommodate misalignment between shafts, reducing stress on connected components and absorbing vibrations, making them ideal for dynamic applications. Rigid couplings, on the other hand, provide a solid connection between shafts, ensuring precise alignment and maximum torque transfer, suitable for static systems. The choice between these types depends on application requirements, including alignment, load, and operational conditions.</p></p>
