<p><ul><li>Nebulizers</li><li>Metered-Dose Inhalers</li><li>Spacers and Valved Holding Chambers</li><li>Dry-Powder Inhalers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerosol drug delivery devices market includes various types designed for effective respiratory therapy. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into mist for inhalation, ideal for severe respiratory conditions. Metered-dose inhalers (MDIs) deliver precise doses of medication using propellants, often favored for their portability. Spacers and valved holding chambers enhance MDI efficacy by allowing medication to disperse before inhalation. Dry-powder inhalers (DPIs) require patient inhalation to aerosolize powdered medication, providing a breath-actuated delivery method, preferred for certain patient demographics.</p></p>
