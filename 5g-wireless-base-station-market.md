<p><p>The 5G wireless base station market encompasses various types designed for distinct coverage and capacity needs. Macro base stations (BS) provide broad coverage for urban and rural areas, ideal for dense populations. Micro BS target specific urban locations, enhancing network capacity in high-demand areas. Pico BS serve smaller areas like buildings or campuses, delivering localized coverage. Femto BS are designed for home or small business use, offering personal network solutions. Together, these types enable efficient and flexible 5G network deployment.</p></p>
