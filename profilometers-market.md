<p><strong>The Profilometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Profilometer</li><li>Non-Contact Profilometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The profilometers market is divided into two primary types: contact and non-contact profilometers. Contact profilometers utilize a stylus that physically touches the surface, providing detailed, accurate topographical data, commonly used in industries requiring precision. Non-contact profilometers, on the other hand, employ optical techniques like laser or white light to measure surface profiles without physical interaction, making them ideal for delicate materials and high-speed applications. Both types serve diverse sectors, including manufacturing, semiconductor, and materials testing.</p></p>
