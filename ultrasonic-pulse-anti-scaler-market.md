<p><p>The Ultrasonic Pulse Anti-Scaler is a technology designed to prevent the formation and accumulation of scale in pipes, boilers, and other water systems by using ultrasonic waves. This technology is attracting attention for its efficiency in reducing maintenance costs, enhancing energy efficiency, and prolonging equipment life.</p><p>The Ultrasonic Pulse Anti-Scaler Market is poised for significant growth, expected to reach a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period.
