<p><p>Anti-osteoporosis therapy and fracture healing encompass several treatment categories. Bisphosphonates inhibit bone resorption, promoting density. Calcitonin regulates calcium and bone metabolism, aiding in fracture healing. Estrogen or hormone replacement therapy replenishes hormones to prevent bone loss. Biologics, including monoclonal antibodies, target specific pathways to enhance bone strength. Anabolics, such as teriparatide, stimulate bone formation for increased density. Together, these therapies support bone health, reduce fracture risk, and enhance the healing process, addressing osteoporosis effectively.</p></p>
