<p><p>The Scratch Remover market comprises two primary types: solid type and emulsion type. Solid type scratch removers are formulated as pastes or sticks, offering targeted application for deeper scratches and providing a thicker consistency for effective results. On the other hand, emulsion type scratch removers are liquid-based solutions that can be easily spread over larger areas, allowing for a smoother finish and quick application. Both types serve distinct user needs, catering to various surfaces and scratch severities.</p></p>
