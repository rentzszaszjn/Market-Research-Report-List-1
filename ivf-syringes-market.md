<p><p>The IVF syringes market primarily includes two types of injection methods: intramuscular and subcutaneous injections. Intramuscular injections deliver hormones or medications directly into the muscle, offering rapid absorption and effectiveness for treatments like ovulation induction. In contrast, subcutaneous injections are administered into the fatty tissue just beneath the skin, providing a less invasive option that can also promote steady medication release. Both methods are essential in fertility treatments, catering to patient comfort and treatment regimens.</p></p>
