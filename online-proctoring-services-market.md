<p><ul><li>Live Proctoring</li><li>Automated Proctoring</li><li>Recorded Proctoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Online proctoring services encompass three primary market types: Live Proctoring, Automated Proctoring, and Recorded Proctoring. Live Proctoring involves real-time monitoring by a proctor during an exam, ensuring integrity through direct supervision. Automated Proctoring utilizes artificial intelligence to monitor test-takers through webcams and screen recordings, flagging suspicious behavior for review. Recorded Proctoring captures the entire testing session for later analysis, allowing for post-examination evaluation of any irregularities while ensuring flexibility for candidates. Each type offers distinct advantages for maintaining examination integrity.</p></p>
