<p><p>The Vision and Navigation System for Autonomous Vehicles comprises various market types, including cameras, sensors, positioning systems, and inertial measurement units (IMUs). Cameras capture visual data for object detection and environmental mapping. Sensors, such as LiDAR and radar, gather distance and obstacle information. Positioning systems utilize GPS and satellite data to determine the vehicle's location, while IMUs track acceleration and rotation, facilitating precise navigation. Together, these components enable autonomous vehicles to navigate safely and efficiently in complex environments.</p></p>
