<p><p>Automatic pallet wrapping machines are essential for securing loads on pallets to protect them during transport and storage. There are two primary types: single-arm and double-arm machines. Single-arm machines feature one arm that rotates around the pallet, wrapping stretch film to secure the goods. Double-arm machines, on the other hand, have two rotating arms that operate simultaneously, enabling faster and more efficient wrapping, especially for larger volumes. Both types enhance productivity and ensure load stability while reducing labor costs.</p></p>
