<p><p>The Microplate Systems market comprises two main types: multi-mode and single-mode microplate systems. Multi-mode microplate systems are versatile instruments capable of performing various assay techniques, such as absorbance, fluorescence, and luminescence, making them suitable for diverse applications in research, diagnostics, and drug discovery. In contrast, single-mode microplate systems focus on one specific assay type, offering specialized performance for tasks like high-throughput screening or enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISAs). Both types cater to unique laboratory needs, enhancing experimental efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
