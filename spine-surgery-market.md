<p><p>The spine surgery market is divided into two main types: Open Spine Surgery and Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery (MISS). Open Spine Surgery involves larger incisions, offering direct access to the spine, suitable for complex procedures but resulting in longer recovery times and increased trauma to surrounding tissue. In contrast, Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery uses smaller incisions, advanced imaging techniques, and specialized instruments, leading to reduced recovery time, less postoperative pain, and quicker return to daily activities, making it increasingly popular among patients and surgeons.</p></p>
