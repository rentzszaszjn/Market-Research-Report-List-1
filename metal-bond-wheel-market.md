<p><strong>The Metal Bond Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin Bond</li><li>Vitrified Bond</li><li>Metal Bond</li><li>Plating Bond</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal Bond Wheel market is categorized into four main types: Resin Bond, Vitrified Bond, Metal Bond, and Plating Bond. Resin Bond wheels offer flexibility and are ideal for delicate materials, while Vitrified Bond wheels provide excellent form retention and hardness for heavy-duty applications. Metal Bond wheels are known for their durability and are used in rough grinding, while Plating Bond wheels have a thin layer of diamond abrasive for precision grinding. Each type caters to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
