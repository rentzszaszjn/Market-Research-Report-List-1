<p><strong>The Hollow-carrier Perforating Guns Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WCP Type</li><li>TCP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hollow-carrier perforating guns are used in oil and gas exploration to create perforations in well casings, facilitating fluid flow. The WCP (Wireline Control Perforating) type offers rapid deployment and retrieval using wireline technology, enabling quick adjustments and controlled operations. In contrast, the TCP (Tubing Conveyed Perforating) type integrates the gun with tubing, allowing for deeper penetration and improved accuracy in perforating, making it suitable for high-pressure environments. Both types enhance efficiency in well completion processes.</p></p>
