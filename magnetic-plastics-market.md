<p><p>The magnetic plastics market comprises two primary types: structured magnetic plastics and compound magnetic plastics. Structured magnetic plastics feature a specific arrangement of magnetic particles integrated into the polymer matrix, optimizing magnetic performance and enhancing physical properties. In contrast, compound magnetic plastics combine a polymer base with additives, such as ferromagnetic materials, to achieve desired magnetic characteristics while maintaining versatility for various applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including electronics, automotive, and consumer goods, driven by the demand for lightweight and efficient materials.</p></p>
