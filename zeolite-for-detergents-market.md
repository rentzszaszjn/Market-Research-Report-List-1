<p><ul><li>Zeolite A</li><li>Zeolite P</li><li>Zeolite X and AX</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zeolites are versatile, crystalline aluminosilicates used in detergent formulations as builders to enhance cleaning efficiency. Zeolite A is highly effective for water softening, preventing calcium and magnesium ions from interfering with detergents. Zeolite P features a high cation-exchange capacity, ideal for boosting stain removal. Zeolite X offers a larger pore structure, enhancing its capacity for ion exchange. Zeolite AX combines properties of A and X, providing effective solubility and performance in both liquid and powdered detergents, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
