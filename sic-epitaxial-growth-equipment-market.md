<p><p>The SiC epitaxial growth equipment market is primarily categorized into three types: CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment, MOCVD (Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment, and others. CVD equipment utilizes chemical reactions to deposit silicon carbide layers on substrates, ensuring uniform thickness and high quality. MOCVD equipment employs organometallic compounds for epitaxial growth, enabling precise control over material composition and layer properties. The "others" category includes alternative methods and technologies used in specialized applications or emerging niche markets within SiC growth.</p></p>
