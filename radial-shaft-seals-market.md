Single lip shaft seals provide basic protection against contaminants and fluid leakage, making them suitable for less demanding environments. In contrast, double lip shaft seals offer enhanced sealing capabilities, featuring an additional lip to better retain lubrication and block contaminants. This makes them ideal for more challenging conditions, such as high speeds or harsh environments, ensuring improved performance and durability in machinery.</p></p>
