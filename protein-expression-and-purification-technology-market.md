<p><p>The Protein Expression and Purification Technology market encompasses various components essential for producing and isolating proteins effectively. Expression vectors are plasmids or other vectors designed to enhance protein production in host cells. Competent cells are specially prepared bacterial or eukaryotic cells capable of taking up the expression vectors and producing the desired proteins. Reagents include enzymes, buffers, and substrates necessary for protein expression and purification processes. Instruments refer to the equipment used for cultivation, purification, and analysis of proteins, enabling efficient research and development.</p></p>
