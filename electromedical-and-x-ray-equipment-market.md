<p><p>The electromedical and X-ray equipment market encompasses three primary types: Digital Radiography (DR), Computed Radiography (CR), and Retrofit Radiography Systems. DR utilizes digital detectors to provide instantaneous images and enhanced image quality, making it highly efficient. CR employs imaging plates that convert X-rays into digital data, allowing for a transition from traditional film-based systems. Retrofit Radiography Systems enhance existing X-ray machines with digital capabilities, offering a cost-effective upgrade path for healthcare facilities to improve diagnostic efficiency and image quality.</p></p>
