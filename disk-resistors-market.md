<p><strong>The Disk Resistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thick Film</li><li>Thin Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disk resistors market is primarily segmented into thick film and thin film categories. Thick film resistors are manufactured using a paste that is screen-printed onto a ceramic substrate, offering high power handling and a cost-effective solution for various applications. In contrast, thin film resistors are created by depositing a thin layer of resistive material, providing superior precision, stability, and lower temperature coefficients. Each type serves distinct needs, catering to different requirements in electronics and industrial applications.</p></p>
