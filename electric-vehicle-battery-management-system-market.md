<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Battery Management System (BMS) market can be categorized into three types: distributed, centralized, and modular systems. </p><p>In a distributed BMS, individual battery cells are monitored independently, enhancing reliability and safety. Centralized systems consolidate management functions in a single unit, simplifying design and reducing costs but may face limitations in scalability. Modular BMS combines elements of both, allowing flexibility and scalability through interconnected modules that can adapt to varying battery configurations, catering to diverse vehicle needs.</p></p>
