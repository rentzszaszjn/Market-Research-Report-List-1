<p><p>The acute bronchitis treatment market encompasses several key types of medications. Antibiotics are used primarily when a bacterial infection is suspected, although most cases are viral. Anti-inflammatory drugs alleviate symptoms by reducing inflammation in the airways. Bronchodilators help relax and widen the air passages, improving airflow and easing breathlessness. Mucolytics work to thin and loosen mucus, making it easier to expel and relieving coughing. Together, these treatments aim to provide symptomatic relief and support recovery.</p></p>
