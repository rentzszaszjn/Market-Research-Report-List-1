<p><p>The sheet membranes market is primarily categorized into three types: PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene), PVDF (polyvinylidene fluoride), and other materials. PTFE membranes are renowned for their chemical resistance and temperature stability, making them ideal for harsh environments. PVDF membranes offer excellent mechanical strength and UV resistance, often used in water treatment and chemical processing. Other materials may include polypropylene and polyethylene, which cater to specialized applications. Each type provides unique properties suited for diverse industrial and commercial uses.</p></p>
