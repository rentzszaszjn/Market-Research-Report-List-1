<p><strong>The Aluminum Metal Matrix Composites Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid State Processing</li><li>Liquid State Processing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aluminum Metal Matrix Composites (AMMC) market can be categorized into two main processing types: solid state processing and liquid state processing. Solid state processing involves techniques such as diffusion bonding or mechanical alloying, where metals are combined without melting, resulting in enhanced mechanical properties and reduced porosity. In contrast, liquid state processing includes methods like casting and stir casting, where aluminum is melted and mixed with reinforcement materials, producing composites with good fluidity and shaping capabilities. Both methods cater to diverse applications across various industries.</p></p>
