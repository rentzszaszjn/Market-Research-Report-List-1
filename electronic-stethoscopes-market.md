<p><p>The electronic stethoscope market is categorized into single-head and dual-head stethoscopes. Single-head stethoscopes feature one diaphragm, providing a straightforward option for basic auscultation, commonly used in primary care settings. In contrast, dual-head stethoscopes include both a diaphragm and a bell, allowing for enhanced versatility by enabling the clinician to listen to a wider range of frequencies. This diversity in design addresses different clinical needs, influencing healthcare professionals' preferences and usage in various medical environments.</p></p>
