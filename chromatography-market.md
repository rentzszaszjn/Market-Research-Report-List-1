<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Chromatography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Chromatography (LC)</li><li>Gas Chromatography (GC)</li><li>Supercritical Fluid Chromatography (SFC)</li><li>Thin Layer Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chromatography market comprises various techniques used for separating mixtures. Liquid Chromatography (LC) utilizes a liquid solvent to separate compounds based on their interactions with a stationary phase. Gas Chromatography (GC) employs vaporized samples and a carrier gas to analyze volatile substances. Supercritical Fluid Chromatography (SFC) combines gas and liquid properties for efficient separations, ideal for complex mixtures. Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) involves a stationary phase on a plate, allowing for quick and simple analysis of compounds in a sample.</p></p>
